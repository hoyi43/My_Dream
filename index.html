04 — Key Features

Designed for the
Life I Want to Live

Every feature carefully chosen to support family, technology, nature, and future growth — all in the heart of a village.

01
Spacious Living Areas
A 16×15 ft living room and open-plan dining create a generous, airy space for the whole family to gather every day.
02
Family-Oriented Design
Parents' room on ground floor, master suite upstairs with a dress room, and a dedicated family lounge for togetherness.
03
Coding & Study Room
A private 12×12 ft room fully dedicated to focus, deep work, learning, and building the future through technology.
04
Open Terrace
A 36×12 ft rooftop terrace for stargazing, morning tea, and breathing fresh village air under an open sky.
05
Green Lawn
A sweeping front lawn and backyard garden to stay rooted in nature, community, and the beauty of village seasons.
06
Modern Architecture
Clean lines, stone cladding, dark steel frames, and large glass panes — a design that stands beautifully for decades.
07
Village Lifestyle
Situated in the village — fresh air, open skies, warm community, and a slower, more meaningful pace of life.
08
Future Expansion Space
A reserved 20×15 ft zone in the backyard for a future workshop, lab, or extra living space as life evolves and grows.
